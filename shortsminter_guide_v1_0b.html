What Is ShortsMinter?

ShortsMinter™ is an AI-powered YouTube Shorts creator that runs entirely on your own PC. You point it at a long-form video, it transcribes the audio, scores every moment for energy and engagement, and automatically finds the best clips to turn into polished 9:16 vertical Shorts.

Everything happens locally on your machine. No cloud. No subscription. No data leaving your PC. Your videos and transcripts stay private.

🧠
AI CLIP SELECTION

Whisper AI transcribes your video and a scoring engine finds your most engaging moments automatically.

🎤
AUTO CAPTIONS

Word-by-word captions with styles, highlights, and karaoke mode — all burned into the video.

😊
EMOJI & TEXT OVERLAYS

Auto or manual emoji overlays. Hook text banners. All customizable per clip.

🚀
FAST GPU EXPORT

NVIDIA GPU encoding via NVENC. Batch export all clips at once with a single button.

📊
AUTO METADATA

Generates titles, descriptions, and tags for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and more.

🔒
100% LOCAL

Runs offline. Your videos never leave your machine. Windows 10 and 11 only.

⚠ Important — GPU Note
GPU acceleration in ShortsMinter is built exclusively for NVIDIA GPUs using NVENC. If you have an AMD or Intel GPU, ShortsMinter will automatically fall back to CPU encoding — it still works, just slower. Make sure "Use GPU encoding" is disabled in your Export settings if you don't have an NVIDIA GPU.
💡 Whisper Model & VRAM
The Whisper base model runs on any system. The medium model needs 8 GB+ VRAM and the large model needs 10 GB+ VRAM. Start with base if you're unsure.

The very first time you launch ShortsMinter, you'll be greeted by the License Activation screen. This only appears once — after successful activation the app opens directly every time.

Activating Your License

  1. 1
    Find Your License KeyCheck your email inbox for the receipt from Gumroad — your license key is included in that email. It will look like a string of letters and numbers.
  2. 2
    Enter the KeyPaste or type your license key into the entry field on the activation screen. Then click ⚡ Activate License.

Your license is stored securely on your PC. On every future launch, ShortsMinter verifies your license quietly in the background. If you're offline, the app will work normally for up to 72 hours using a local cache. After that a connection is required.

Editor Page — The Full Workflow

The Editor page is where all the magic happens. Work through the numbered steps from top to bottom — the app is designed to guide you through the workflow in order.

① Load Your Video

Click 📂 Load Video and select any video file from your PC. ShortsMinter accepts common formats including MP4, MKV, MOV and AVI. Once loaded, the app shows the video filename, duration, resolution and frame rate.

💡 Best Source Videos
Long-form content works best — gaming sessions, podcasts, commentary, vlogs, reaction videos, tutorials. The longer the source, the more clips the AI can find. Aim for at least 10–20 minutes for the best results.

② AI Settings

These four settings control how the AI finds and selects clips from your video. Configure these before clicking Analyse.

SettingWhat It Controls
AI EngineChooses the clip-selection brain. Local only uses a built-in scoring engine — fast, no API key needed. You can also connect Claude or ChatGPT APIs for even smarter selection.
Transcribe (Whisper Model)The model used to transcribe speech. base is fast and accurate for most content. small and medium are more accurate but slower. See Section 2 for VRAM requirements.
Number of ClipsHow many Shorts to extract. Check Auto to let the app calculate the maximum possible for your video length and clip duration.
Clip DurationTarget length for each Short in seconds. YouTube Shorts must be 60 seconds or under. 30–45 seconds is the sweet spot for engagement.

AI Engine Options Explained

EngineWhat It Does
Local only (fast, no API key)Built-in energy and engagement scoring. No API key needed. Great starting point for most users.
Local + Claude APICombines local scoring with Claude AI for smarter clip ranking. Requires a Claude API key.
Local + ChatGPT APISame as above but uses OpenAI's ChatGPT. Requires an OpenAI API key.
Local + Both APIsUses all three — local scoring plus both AI engines. Most thorough selection.
Claude + ChatGPT (no local)Skips local scoring entirely and uses both AI APIs only.

③ Supercharge Whisper

The 🔬 Supercharge Whisper button unlocks advanced speaker separation (diarization) using a free HuggingFace account token. This is completely optional — the app works great without it. If you process content with multiple speakers and want them labelled separately in transcripts, set this up first. Follow the instructions in the popup to get your free token.

④ Manual Clip Timing

If you want to manually define exactly where a clip starts and ends instead of using AI selection, use the manual timing fields. Enter a start time and end time in HH:MM:SS or seconds format, then click Add Manual Clip. Manual clips are added directly to the Clip Queue and skip the AI analysis step.

⑤ Analyse & Find Best Clips

Once you've configured settings ①–④, click 🧠 ANALYSE & FIND BEST CLIPS. This is the main AI button. Here's what happens:

  1. 1
    Audio ExtractionShortsMinter extracts the audio track from your video.
  2. 2
    TranscriptionWhisper AI transcribes every word spoken, with timestamps. This is the longest step — progress shows in the Live Log.
  3. 3
    Clip ScoringThe app scores every possible window of your video for energy, reactions, hype moments, tips, and engagement.
  4. 4
    AI Ranking (if enabled)If you selected a Claude or ChatGPT engine, those APIs rank the top candidates and select the best.
  5. 5
    Clips Added to QueueThe best clips appear in the Clip Queue on the right side of the Editor, ready for you to review and export.
💡 While Analysing
Watch the Live Log at the bottom right for detailed progress. Transcription time depends on your Whisper model and hardware — base on a good GPU takes 1–3 minutes per hour of video.

⚠ GPU Encoding Warning — NVIDIA Only
GPU acceleration is built exclusively for NVIDIA GPUs. If you have an AMD or Intel GPU, disable GPU encoding in the Export tab to avoid errors. If your screen flickers briefly during export (TDR event), this is Windows restarting the GPU driver — it is not a hardware failure. The current clip may need to be re-queued. This is normal under heavy GPU load.

Export All Clips

When you're happy with your clip queue and all settings, click 🚀 EXPORT ALL CLIPS at the bottom of the screen. ShortsMinter processes every queued clip one by one, burning in captions, emoji, text overlays, and applying all your export settings. Watch the Live Log for real-time progress on each clip.

Click ⛔ STOP at any time to stop after the current clip finishes. Already-completed clips are kept — only the remaining queue is cancelled.

Live Log

The Live Log at the bottom right of the Editor shows timestamped messages for every action — clip detection, transcription progress, export steps, errors, and completions. If something goes wrong, the log is the first place to look. You can copy the log from Settings → Support → Copy App Log to paste into a bug report.

💡 Filename Settings
You can customize how exported clips are named. In Settings, the Filename section lets you choose a mode and set a prefix. Clips can be named with a date, format tag, duration, and sequence number — all configurable with drag-and-drop chip ordering.

Voice Profile

The Voice Profile feature lets you record a short sample of your voice so Whisper can personalize its transcription model for the way you speak. This is entirely optional but can noticeably improve transcription accuracy, especially if you have a regional accent, speak fast, or use a lot of domain-specific language.

Recording Your Voice Profile

  1. 1
    Open the Voice ProfileOn the Home page, click 🎙 Set Up Voice Profile, or go to Settings → Data & Cache → Record Voice Profile.
  2. 2
    Read the PromptA short paragraph of text is shown on screen. Read it aloud naturally — don't rush. The recording takes about 20–30 seconds.
  3. 3
    SaveWhen done, click Stop and Save. Your voice profile is stored locally. Whisper will use it to improve accuracy on your next analysis.

Deleting Your Voice Profile

To remove your voice profile, go to Settings → Data & Cache → Delete Profile. This permanently removes both the recording and the speech pattern cache. Whisper reverts to standard mode immediately.

💡 Tip
Re-record your Voice Profile any time — for example if you get a new microphone or notice transcription accuracy has drifted. The new recording replaces the old one.

What Makes a Great Short

ShortsMinter scores clips based on energy, reactions, hype language, funny moments, and useful tips. To help the AI find the best clips from your content, consider these things:

⚡
HIGH ENERGY MOMENTS

Clips with hype reactions, celebrations, wins, funny moments and strong emotions score highest. Gaming and commentary content is ideal.

💡
TIPS & TUTORIALS

Moments where you say "pro tip," "here's how," or "you should" are scored as high-value clips for tutorial and educational content.

📢
CLEAN SENTENCES

The AI prefers clips that end on a complete sentence. Make sure your source audio is clear and you're not trailing off mid-sentence.

Caption Editing — Fix Whisper Mistakes

If Whisper misheard a word — especially proper nouns, names, or slang — select the clip in the queue and click ✏ Captions to open the caption editor. Fix any incorrect words and click Save. The corrected captions will be used on the next export. You don't need to re-run the whole analysis.

What Is a TDR? (Screen Flicker During Export)

If your screens briefly go black and come back during export, this is called a TDR — Timeout Detection & Recovery. Windows detected that your GPU driver stopped responding and restarted it automatically. This is not a hardware failure and is normal under heavy GPU load during video encoding. The clip being processed at that moment may fail and need to be re-queued. If TDRs happen very frequently, check your GPU temperatures and make sure your NVIDIA drivers are up to date.

💡 Reduce TDR Risk
Close other GPU-intensive applications (games, video players, browsers with hardware acceleration) during a large export batch. This gives ShortsMinter the most headroom.
